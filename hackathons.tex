
\cventry{2015}{IdEar Team Member}{Hackathon: \href{http://medhacks.org/}{MedHacks 1.0}}{}{
Showed as a proof of concept that ears could be used as biometric markers in a global health framework.  The target was areas with poor to no registries of people in which pictures of ears could be used to distinguish community members when other demographic information was not unique.  Implemented a MATLAB implementation of an SVM to classify people based on image of ear and scale invariant feature transform (SIFT) features. In top 10 of 30 teams}{}{}
